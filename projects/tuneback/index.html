Role: UX Designer & Front-End Developer

Task: Develop a responsive website

Tools: Paper prototyping, HTML & CSS

Team: One-Man Team

Assignment

This web development project was assigned by the academy, in the division: Tech. We were assigned to design and develop a responsive website using HTML & CSS.

For this project, I tried to create a platform where you can give and get feedback on music productions.

Process

One of the first steps was to make paper prototypes to envision my idea's. By making sketches, you will encounter problems that you can counter by iterating on the design or by making a whole different sketch. By selecting the best iterations a "final" wireframe was imagined that could be tested to make improvements.
