Assignment

This research project was assigned by the academy in, the division: Human. We were assigned to gather qualitative insights on a certain behavior of people by observing and interviewing them. The obtained meaningful insights needed to be visualized on a poster.

We were interested in observing and researching shame. Why people are ashamed and why people act differently under certain circumstances.

Process

Firstly information was gathered by observation. Trying to read peculiar behavior in the center of Breda. After observing people we tried to gather meaningful insights by interviewing. We collected all the data and analyzed them by using theory we received from our academy.

By making sketches, we tried to rearrange the information in other forms. By going through this process, we tried to discover the purpose of shame and where it came from. The next step was to gather all the sketches and make one final poster out of it.
